About

I am a PhD student at UC Berkeley in Environmental Science, Policy and Management studying fire ecology using remote sensing. I am co-advised by Dr. Van Butsic, who studies land use and land use change, and Dr. Scott Stephens, who researches wildland fire. I am interested in Improved Forest Management carbon offsets in California’s cap-and-trade market and questions of environmental justice in wildfire recovery.

I have a Bachelor of Science from UC Berkeley, where I majored in Conservation and Resource Studies and Society and Environment and minored in Forestry, Public Policy, and Geospatial Information Systems.

Want to chat about Remote Sensing, Forest Policy, GIS, or Wildfire Ecology? Send me a message.
